Why we do what we do. Most homes in Nairobi although supplied water by the county government, still don’t trust the purity of the water. Most homes resort to using bottled water and or boiled water for drinking purposes. This comes as an added expenses on the household either by increased fuel consumption for the boiling water or buying of water dispenser and its bottles. Other homes use borehole water which mostly is found to be hard water there by depending solely on bottled water for drinking and not to mention the damage of the highly alkaline water on our clothes and utensils. This is an added cost which can be drastically reduced to negligible levels. Through a combination of different water filters, Ourvid has come up with our brand machine that will take care of this problem.

Ourvid Enterprises is a business setup to provide water purification systems to both domestic and commercial entities. We are currently a partnership business with its office located at Woodlands Business Park, 2nd floor suite 8. Kiambere road, off upper hill road upper hill Nairobi. Our main goal is to develop a sustainable water purification industry and create a one stop shop for all residential and commercial needs in regards to purification systems and their all required spare parts. Ourvid’s mission is to segment the market for purified water by offering desalination of hard water at the point of use by providing right water filters.

"VIVID" is our brand name with the narrative "TRUE TO LIFE"

The main filter compination to used will be:

  • Reverse Osmosis Membrane (RO)
  • Ultrafine depth filters(UDF)
  • Chlorine, taste and odor filters (CTO)
  • Granular activated carbon filters (GAC)
  • Polypropylene filters (PP)
  • Ultraviolet sterilizer (UV)

    • These filters combined create an optimized product (VIVID) that will give you purified water in your house or office ready to drink. VIVID purifying machines can desalinate water for both domestic and commercial purposes. Makes it safe and tasty for drinking
